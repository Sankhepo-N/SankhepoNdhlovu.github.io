Biography

Sankhepo Ndhlovu is a dedicated and passionate individual with a strong background in Mathematics. Inspired by personal experiences and witnessing health disparities, she is driven to make a meaningful impact in the field of public health. With a focus on analyzing large datasets and applying statistical methods, Sankhepo aims to contribute to medical and health-related research, particularly in marginalized populations. Her goal is to understand disease patterns, evaluate treatment effectiveness, and address health disparities to improve healthcare outcomes. With a strong analytical mindset and a commitment to advancing public health, Sankhepo is eager to pursue a Master of Science degree in Biostatistics and make a positive difference in the field.

Research on CitiBike Program Utilization and Trends
~SAS Analytics~

This research project aims to analyze and derive insights from the trip data of the Citi Bike bike-sharing program in New York City. By investigating various aspects of the data, the study intends to shed light on how the bike-sharing system is used and provide valuable insights for understanding user behavior and preferences.
